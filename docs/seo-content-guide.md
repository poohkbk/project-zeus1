# SEO Content Guide

## Titles

Use one clear title per page. Put the primary search phrase near the front, then describe the actual service or topic.

Good:

`청주민사변호사 | 대여금·계약·손해배상·부동산 | 법률사무소 제우`

Avoid:

- Repeating the same keyword unnaturally
- Promising results
- Making five pages with the same body and only changing the keyword

## Descriptions

Descriptions should summarize the actual page in one or two sentences. Mention the case type, issue, and what the user can confirm.

Avoid:

- Lists of keywords only
- Exaggerated advertising phrases
- Duplicate descriptions across pages

## Headings

Use exactly one H1. The H1 is the main page question or promise. Use H2 for major sections and H3 for cards or detailed topics.

## GEO Answer Paragraphs

At the top of important pages, answer the user question directly in one to three sentences. The answer must be visible to users, not hidden only for crawlers.

Example:

`민사소송은 억울함만으로 진행하기 어렵습니다. 청구 원인, 입증자료, 상대방 재산과 집행 가능성을 함께 검토해야 실제 해결 가능성을 판단할 수 있습니다.`

## Image Alt Text

Describe the image briefly and naturally.

Good:

`법률사무소 제우 강병권 변호사`

Avoid:

`청주변호사 청주변호사 청주변호사`

Decorative images may use empty alt text.

## Legal Information

Legal content should separate general information from advice for a specific case. Do not guarantee lawsuit results or imply that the same outcome applies to every case.

## Updates

Update `dateModified` only when content actually changes. Do not change dates only to make content look recent.
